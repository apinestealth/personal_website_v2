About Me
I am a product designer interested in making our technological infrastructure more useable, beautiful, and fun. I am deeply committed to the idea that the future of technology is more just and equitable.
Background
I'm originally from Alabama, but moved to the West Coast for an education. At Stanford University, I took computer science and design classes in addition to picking up a degree in Comparative Studies in Race and Ethnicity and completing an honors thesis about the legacy of race, power, and wealth in my hometown. I am a systems-thinker and storyteller through and through.
