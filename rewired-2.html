Rewired is a technology and society magazine that publishes three issues yearly. As an in-house designer during my senior year at Stanford I created graphics to go along with articles. I met with the writers before publication to develop an understanding of the piece and then created a design, sometimes with several options, that fit within the overall aesthetic theme of each issue.
